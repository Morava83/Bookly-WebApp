Bookly

Bookly is a McGill booking application for office hours, meeting requests, and group scheduling.

Students can reserve appointments with professors and teaching assistants without relying on long email chains.

Owners: @mcgill.ca accounts create, activate, and manage booking slots.
Users: @mail.mcgill.ca and @mcgill.ca accounts can request and reserve active slots.
Booking types: request a meeting, group meeting scheduling, and recurring office hours.

Log in

Sign in with your McGill email to open your dashboard, review appointments, and book available office hours.

How Bookly works

  • Only McGill email addresses can register for the application.
  • Owners use @mcgill.ca accounts to create private slots, activate public slots, and share invitation URLs.
  • After login, users can see appointments, book open slots, cancel bookings, and email slot owners through mailto links.
